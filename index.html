The SARCS integrated Climate Change (CC) and adaptive initiatives in both program and project planning and implementation whilst taking into consideration gendered vulnerabilities. Disaster Management

The central goal of the SARCS disaster management programme is to ensure that the vulnerability of communities to natural and man-made disasters has reduced through an effective disaster management system of the Red Cross movement and better coordination between the local authorities and other key actors in the country.
